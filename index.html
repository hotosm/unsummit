HOT UNSUMMIT 2022



Read our Announcement

For the past 7 years, the HOT Summit has brought together local and global communities, humanitarian and development agencies, academics, volunteers and contributors, and government and private sector partners to share, explore, and discuss how to leverage OpenStreetMap and open mapping for social impact and change.

The last two HOT Summits were online, due to Covid, and this has given us the chance to reflect on how to do this. In the next 12 months, we will not hold a central HOT Summit, but instead will invest the time, money and other resources in supporting and collaborating on a range of conferences and community events, bringing the spirit of the Summit to multiple new places and hundreds of new people!

To read more about our new approach to the summit, please see our recent news post.
